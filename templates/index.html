Introduction

Let's bring games and gamification to Slack. With Brain games you able to play the classical games like tic-tac-toe and hangman.

The idea is to introduce more games and gamification in chatting applications. In future the plan is to have also multiplayer games. Want to contribute, share feedback and ideas come and join the slack channel.

Tic Tac Toe

Tic-tac-toe (also known as Noughts and crosses or Xs and Os) is a paper-and-pencil game for two players, X and O, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3Ã—3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game. - Wikipedia

Quick intro how to play this in Slack version:

  • Start a game /ttt start
  • To make your move on cell[1-9] /ttt move 5, this will make move in the center spot
  • The AI makes the move after you and returns the current game state
  • If you want to query the current game state /ttt current
  • To get info about available commands /ttt help

Hangman

Hangman is a paper and pencil guessing game for two or more players. One player thinks of a word, phrase or sentence and the other tries to guess it by suggesting letters or numbers, within a certain number of guesses. - Wikipedia

Quick intro how to play this in Slack version:

  • Start a game /hng start
  • To make your guess /hng guess a(possible values are between a-z)
  • If you want to query the current game state /hng current
  • To get info about available commands /hng help
